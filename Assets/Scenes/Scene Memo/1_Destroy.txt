1_Destroy

Allow player to destroy object tagged with "Destroyable Obstacle" and be destroyed by "Hazard".

<Destroy>
- the object can only be destroyed by bullet, otherwise player cannot pass
- when hit by bullet, target object can be moved and be destroyed after a time delay

<Being Destroyed>
- the player can be destroyed when passing through hazard object
- when passing through hazard, palyer changes colour and will then be destroyed after a time delay
